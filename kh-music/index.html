KRISTA HECKMANN

MEZZO SOPRANO

Kentucky native, Krista Heckmann, graduated from the University of Louisville in 2016 with a degree in Vocal Preformace. She enjoys performing and teaching voice lessons. She is the Children's Choir Co-Director at Second Presbyterian as well as a section leader in the choir. She's a member of Kentcuky Opera and has been in numerous shows. Visit the Resumé section for more information. (Click anywhere for less information)

 
Voice lessons are offered to students of all ages in 10 week terms followed by a recital

 

In 2015, Krista joined the octet at Second Presbyterian. After joining she was asked to Co-direct Children's Choir. She's also a vocal coach at Folsom Academy.

Krista graduated with a Bachelors Degree in Vocal Preformace from the University of Louisville in 2016. During her time at UofL she preformed in Dialogues of the Carmelites (Chorus), Don Giovanni (Chorus), Old Maid and the Theif (Miss Todd), and Magic Flute (Third Lady).

Krista has been a memeber as Kentucky Opera since 2013, where she's performed in such shows as La Boheme (Chorus), Romeo et Julliette (Chorus), Woman in Morocco (Woman 5), Macbeth (Witch Chorus), Madame Butterfly (Geisha), and Abduction from the Seraglio (Chorus).

In 2013, she began preforming with Thompson Street Opera in the shows Opera (Lady 3), Cephalophora (Chorus) , Cosmic Ray and the Amazing Chris (Dr. Terra/Chorus), Intoxication: America's Love Affair with Oil (Sienna/Spectator 2/Chorus) , The Final Battle for Love (Starla).
